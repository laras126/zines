I feel depleted today
5/9/20

Sleep has not been a friend to me for the past three nights. Not just one night, three nights in a row. The night after the first night, its like, "Well, I am so tired, I will definitely sleep tonight."

And then I lay down to sleep and something happens and I'm like, "What if it happens again...no it wouldn't, I'm so tired...".

And then it happens again.

And then the next night, after two nights of terrible sleep, it's like, "Wow, I am so, so tired, I will definitely sleep tonight."

And then I lay down to sleep and something happens and I think, "No, it couldn't happen again, could it? Not 3 nights in a row...I am so tired..." and then my chest feels tight and I think "Oh my god, it's happening again" and then I feel really hot, not sweaty, just hot, and like my eyes are open even though they are closed.

I try to count my breaths and control my thoughts. Work thoughts and sex thoughts keep creeping in, and I try to use the meditation tactics and turn my focus back to counting. Then I feel thirsty. I drink some water and roll back onto the "getting ready to fall asleep" side. Then I roll over to the "ready fall asleep" side and I feel hungry.

I try counting my breaths again, then I think, "whatever, if I'm going to be laying here for hours I'm going to think about what I want to think about." Which is sex. Then I feel turned on and even more awake. Then I feel angry and think, "FUCK THIS SHIT WHY THE FUCK IS THIS HAPPENENING". I'm frustrated. I don't understand it.

I know I will survive – I've given a conference talk on zero hours of sleep before – and it's not impossible that the day might even be a good day - but it also might be the worst day. I had fun plans for tomorrow, and now they are ruined because I will be a shell of my normal self. I wanted to go for a long run and have a wonderful, restful weekend day, but it's all been ruined by this fucking sleep shit. And I feel really sad and I mourn the wonderful, restful weekend I will not have.

After two to three cycles of this, interrupted by bathroom breaks and going to the other room to write in my jounral, I fall asleep. Then I wake up at 7am and think, "fuck this shit". I doze off for an hour or so. Then I wake up and just lay there. Feeling like I did when I went to sleep.
